About Us

Welcome To Rotaract RBU

With a noble desire to take the lead for the upliftment of society, empowering women and youth, we Rotaract club of Shri Ramdeobaba college of Engineering and Management from Nagpur strives forth with novel point of view that will pave the path for for the club to reach greater heights, and a better community .

We strive for excellence, diligent work culture and ought to prepare fellow rotaractors as future leaders and compassionate citizens.

Our Events

Few of Our Notable Events

  • Fit Street

    Join us on Fit Street, where we promote healthy living through various fitness activities and challenges.

  • Resume Building Session

    Enhance your career prospects by attending our Resume Building Session, designed to help you create a standout resume.

  • Spread Happines

    Participate in our Spread Happiness event, where we bring joy to the community through various fun and engaging activities.

  • Green Mourya

    Join our Green Mourya initiative to promote environmental sustainability through eco freindly Ganpati Bappa Idol making.

  • Stationary Donation

    Contribute to our Stationary Donation drive, where we collect and distribute stationery to underprivileged students.

  • NMC Cleanlines Drive

    Take part in our NMC Cleanliness Drive and help us make our community a cleaner and healthier place to live.

  • Garbha Night

    Celebrate the festival of Navratri with us at Garbha Night, filled with traditional music, dance, and cultural activities.

About Rotary

Check this out

  • What is Rotary?

    Rotary International is one of the largest service organizations in the world. The mission of Rotary is to "provide service to others, promote integrity, and advance world understanding, goodwill, and peace through the fellowship of business, professional, and community leaders".

  • Rotary in RBU

    Chartered on 15th February 2012, Sponsored by Rotary Club of Nagpur Mihan Town, we align our interest with District 3030 to be a better version of ourselves.

  • Unleash your potential

    Are you ready to make a real difference in your community? Join Rotaract RCOEM and become part of a global network of young leaders dedicated to service, friendship, and personal growth. As a Rotaractor, you'll have the opportunity to: Lead Projects, Network Globally, Develop Skills, Create Memories, Inspire Change.
